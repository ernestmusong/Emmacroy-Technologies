Web development simply involves the creation and maintenance of websites. We cover both front-end and back-end web development referred to as full stack web development


Front-end web development is the development of the graphical user interface of a website, so that users can view and interact with that website.

Front-end web development involves what a user sees on the screen when they open a specific URL or link. For example, when you login to your facebook account, the images, text, and buttons are all front-end web development

At Emmacroy Technologies, we teach HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Redux for front-end development.


Back-end web development is the development of the server-side of a website. It involves everything you canâ€™t see on a website. For example, when you create an account on facebook, the data is stored in a database, and when you login to your account, the data is retrieved. The storing and retrieving of that data is the back-end development.
